The Historic City of Gates 🏛

📍 About the City

Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar is a historic city in Maharashtra known for its Mughal-era architecture, vibrant culture and proximity to the world-famous Ajanta and Ellora caves.

🌍 Famous Tourist Places

🗿 Ajanta Caves

Ancient Buddhist cave paintings and sculptures.

🏞 Ellora Caves

Rock-cut temples representing three religions.

🏰 Bibi Ka Maqbara

Known as the Taj of the Deccan.

🛕 Grishneshwar Temple

One of the 12 Jyotirlinga temples dedicated to Lord Shiva.

🏯 Devgiri Fort

A magnificent hill fort famous for its strong defense architecture.

🙏 Bhadra Maruti Temple

A famous temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman located near Khuldabad.

💧 Panchakki

A historic water mill built in the Mughal era.

🌳 Siddharth Garden

A beautiful garden and zoo popular among families and tourists.

📜 History of the City

🏰 Mughal Era

The city was founded in 1610 by Malik Ambar and later became an important administrative center during the Mughal period.

⚔ Maratha Influence

The region played an important role during the Maratha Empire and was named after the brave Maratha ruler Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj.

🏙 Modern Development

Today Sambhajinagar is an industrial, educational and tourism hub attracting visitors from around the world.
