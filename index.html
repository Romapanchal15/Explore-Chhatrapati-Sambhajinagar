Welcome to Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar

Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar is one of the most historically significant cities in Maharashtra. The city is known for its rich heritage, Mughal architecture, ancient caves, and cultural importance. It serves as the gateway to the famous Ajanta and Ellora caves.

Tourists from around the world visit the city to explore its monuments, temples, gardens, and historical forts. The city offers a unique combination of history, culture, spirituality, and natural beauty.

Why Visit the City?

  • Home to UNESCO World Heritage Sites
  • Ancient Buddhist rock-cut caves
  • Historic Mughal monuments
  • Beautiful temples and gardens
  • Rich culture and heritage
  • Nearby forts and scenic locations
